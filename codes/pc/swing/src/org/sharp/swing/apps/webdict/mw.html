linter


lint·er

noun \ˈlin-tər\

Definition of LINTER

1
: a machine for removing linters
2
plural : the fuzz of short fibers that adheres to cottonseed after ginning

First Known Use of LINTER

circa 1889

Rhymes with LINTER

hinter, minter, Pinter, printer, sinter, splinter, sprinter, squinter, tinter, winter
